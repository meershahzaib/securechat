Security Features

Military Grade Protection

Using the advanced AES-256 encryption standard, the same technology trusted by governments and military organizations worldwide to protect classified information.

Zero Knowledge

Our platform is designed with zero-knowledge architecture, meaning we cannot read your messages or access your encryption keys under any circumstances.

No Logs

We maintain no records of your conversations. Messages are never stored on our servers once delivered, leaving absolutely no trail of your communications.

Secure Authentication

Multi-factor authentication and advanced identity verification protects your account while maintaining your privacy.

Self-Destructing Messages

Set your messages to automatically delete after they've been read or after a specific time period for enhanced security.

Anti-Screenshot Protection

Advanced protection prevents screenshots of sensitive conversations and notifies you if someone attempts to capture your chat.

Regular Security Audits

We conduct regular security audits and vulnerability assessments to ensure our system remains secure and up-to-date.

DDoS Protection

Our robust infrastructure includes advanced DDoS protection measures to keep your communication uninterrupted and secure.
